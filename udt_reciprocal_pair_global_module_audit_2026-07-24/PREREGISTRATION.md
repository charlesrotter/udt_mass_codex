## Whole question

On a genuinely toric angular region, the complete metric and integral
lattice derive a two-element shortest-character set at a generic systolic
wall. Reciprocity exchanges the two members at the symmetric control.

This audit asks, without choosing either member:

> Does the full reciprocal pair define a smaller global geometric reduction
> with intrinsic transition laws and invariants, or does its unimodular span
> merely recover the complete rank-two character local system? Under which
> exact additional global data can an exchange lift, fixed circle, quotient,
> or conditional Hopf bundle be defined?

The conditional Hopf comparison occurs only after the module and completion
census. It cannot retroactively select an input.

## Exact bounded regime

The algebraic universe is:

1. rank-two integral character lattices `Lambda*`;
2. positive determinant-one dual metrics `Q`;
3. generic two-way shortest walls, three-way vertices, and tie-free chambers;
4. `GL(2,Z)` lattice transitions and the complete signed-permutation
   stabilizer of an unordered unoriented basis;
5. identity, sign, involutive swap, order-four swap, shear, hyperbolic, cap,
   rank-loss, and nonintegrable transition/termination cases;
6. the twelve already registered finite-cell completion families; and
7. the already conditional reciprocal-toric `S3`/Hopf witness.

This is not a census of arbitrary future nonlocal laws, field equations,
actions, matter carriers, or all manifolds.

## Falsification and certification

The frozen `FALSIFICATION_CONTRACT.tsv` is binding. In particular, the audit
fails if it:

- treats a two-way co-shortest pair as non-unimodular;
- calls the pair a proper rank-one or proper rank-two submodule when its
  span is the full lattice;
- extends two-way co-shortest status into a chamber or uniquely through a
  three-way vertex;
- omits a signed/order-four lift or a completion family;
- lets arbitrary `GL(2,Z)` monodromy preserve an unordered splitting;
- derives a fixed circle from an order-four exchange;
- erases the index-two integral relation between exchange eigensublattices;
- obtains a Hopf class without the required global toric and freeness data;
- calls a bundle quotient a selected carrier field; or
- changes any action, matter, density, mass, boundary, frozen, or navigation
  verdict.

Certification requires deterministic production replay, an independent
implementation importing no production module, exercised mutations,
source-identity replay, all twelve completions, and repository gates.
