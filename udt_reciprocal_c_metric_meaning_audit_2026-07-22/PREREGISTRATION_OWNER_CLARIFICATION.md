Date: 2026-07-22  
Applies before any production algebra is run.

Charles adds that UDT includes a **GR-like reciprocity of observational frames**.

For this audit the minimum owner meaning is:

- no observational frame is privileged by the macro UDT laws;
- the reciprocal clock–spatial-measure relation must have the same physical content when described
  by different observers; and
- a frame change may alter components without altering the underlying geometric relation.

This does **not** silently import:

- Einstein field equations or an EH action;
- GR's full observer mechanics as a native UDT derivation;
- universal local Lorentz behavior at every extreme value of `phi`;
- gravity–acceleration equivalence outside any later derived regime;
- a preferred foliation, clock congruence, spatial axis, or material rest frame; or
- a micro-UDT frame theorem.

The exact Lorentz boosts used in the audit are therefore comparison controls for ordinary-regime
local frame covariance and for the algebraic meaning of “same diagonal components in every frame.”
The banked conclusion must be tensorial: components may transform, and a solution may contain
distinguished geometric fields, without making one observer fundamental in the laws.

The certification contract is extended as follows:

1. a claimed observer-neutral metric relation must transform covariantly;
2. keeping a nontrivial reciprocal distortion numerically diagonal and unchanged in every boosted
   coframe is not frame reciprocity and must fail the exact commutator test;
3. a solution-specific clock/depth structure is not automatically a preferred frame in the laws;
4. a relational observer/displacement rule must still assemble consistently into one four-metric
   before it can be called the complete metric; and
5. no result may infer micro-frame equivalence from the macro owner statement.
