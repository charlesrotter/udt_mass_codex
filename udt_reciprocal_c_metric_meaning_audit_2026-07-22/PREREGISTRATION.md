## Certification and falsification contract

The claim “the two postulates derive the complete observer-neutral `3+1` metric” fails unless one
four-tensor is obtained independently of an unregistered observer, displacement direction,
transverse completion, chart, or coframe gauge.

The claim “a preferred rank-two subbundle is required” fails if the postulates are consistently
realized as a relational measurement rule and the complete metric transforms covariantly without a
fixed plane field. A merely pairwise formula does not count as a completed four-metric.

The claim “no preferred frame” fails only if the **laws** require one universal observer. A
solution-specific static congruence, gradient, or material rest frame is not by itself a preferred
frame in the laws.

Any nontrivial reciprocal distortion that is asserted to be identical and diagonal in every
Lorentz-related observer coframe must pass exact boost compatibility. Failure will be recorded as a
failure of that candidate meaning, not of UDT Reciprocity.
