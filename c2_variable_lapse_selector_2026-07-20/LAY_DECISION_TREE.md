   No, not in the regular compact static no-shift branch.
2. **Why not?**
   Changing only the clock changes its ratio to the spatial rulers. The conditional `C^2` equation
   detects that mismatch as conformal curvature.
3. **Can a nonconstant clock factor ever remain?**
   Yes, when the same factor multiplies the entire spatial measuring system too. Then it is the same
   pre-scale geometry under CSN, not a newly selected physical clock profile.
4. **Did this prove there is no UDT time dilation?**
   No. It classified one static diagonal branch. Shift/connection terms, acceleration, genuine time
   dependence, singular horizons, and the physical boundary remain open.
5. **Did it pick the size or absolute clock rate?**
   No. Common scale remains free.
6. **Did it derive matter or use electron mass?**
   No. There is still no derived carrier, material action, source, or mass equation.
