## Whole question

Does the registered reciprocal UDT metric, with its angular sector left free rather than silently
rounded or torified, force a smooth conformally natural null line through either:

1. a nontrivial null/eikonal `d phi`; or
2. a uniquely repeated Weyl principal null direction of Petrov type II, III, or N?

The audit tests the two exact local candidates retained by the preceding projective-transport
selector. It does not assume that a locally selected null line is the carrier, geodesic everywhere,
globally smooth, periodic, or boundary-complete.

## Observing or targeting

conformally flat, algebraically general, algebraically special, degenerate, and type-transition

## Metric-led versus template-led

The primary family is metric-led:

```text
g=-exp(-2 phi) dt^2 + exp(2 phi) dr^2 + q_AB dx^A dx^B,
```

with positive spatial `q_AB` and no initially selected angular topology or periods. It is a local
representative of the registered reciprocal temporal/parallel block, not a complete on-shell metric.

Spherical/constant-curvature `q_AB=R^2 gamma_AB`, diagonal anisotropic transverse warps, staticity,
and time-live radial reductions are bounded comparison subfamilies. They are `CHOSE / CONDITIONAL`
ansätze used to classify possibilities and construct counterfamilies, not UDT derivations.

## Falsification and certification contract

A unique conformal-null-line theorem passes only if the complete registered metric foundation,
without a spherical/anisotropic template or field equation, forces either:

- nonzero `d phi` with `g^-1(d phi,d phi)=0` on the required complete domain; or
- one smooth highest-multiplicity Weyl PND, with the same selected line through all allowed angular,
  mirror, and type-transition strata.

The line must be conformally natural and no observer, sign convention, seed, Petrov class, action,
or boundary value may be inserted to select it.

An underdetermination result requires exact reciprocal kinematic counterfamilies with different
`d phi` causal/Petrov classifications, plus explicit acknowledgement that absent action and boundary
equations prevent certifying them as complete on-shell universes.

Every load-bearing curvature component and root multiplicity receives runnable exact algebra and an
independent implementation. Mutation catches must reject: dropping positive angular-gradient terms;
calling a static nonzero gradient null; promoting the eikonal PDE to a founding identity; treating
Petrov D's pair as unique; treating O as a selected ray; deleting the Petrov-I counterfamily; using a
GR field equation to make a PND geodesic; skipping seal/type transitions; or claiming carrier/cap/
action closure.
