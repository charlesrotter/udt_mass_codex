   Current UDT supplies neither an invariant `sigma` nor its transformation under local CSN.
   Consequently `L_AB/sigma=lambda` and every `sigma -> phi,F` rate are conditional family controls,
   not a physical scale theorem.
2. Swapping observer order `A <-> B`, reversing the signed local field value `phi -> -phi`, and
   transforming a complete metric configuration under a putative reciprocal reflection are three
   different operations. Only observer-order reversal is used in the connector comparison. No
   complete-metric `phi -> -phi` symmetry or microscopic observer theorem is assumed.
3. `F`, angular/path data, shift, and `sigma -> phi` are free-and-explored audit parameters because
   current sources leave them `OPEN_NOT_SELECTED`. This does not assert that they are physically free
   fields; a future realization law may constrain them.

quadratic readout, physical CSN representative, time orientation, observer congruence, slicing, and
connector. There are no *unacknowledged habit-pins* after these corrections, but the controls are not
premise-free.
