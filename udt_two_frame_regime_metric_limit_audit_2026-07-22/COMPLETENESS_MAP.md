# Completeness map

This package is one exact kinematic tile. It is not a solution-space solve or a complete time-live
theory.

## Covered

- the founded reciprocal diagonal control with `D=exp(-phi)` and explicit observed `c_E`;
- arbitrary positive lapse `N`, positive connector line factor `H`, and signed tangent shift `B` in
  an exact stationary one-dimensional metric reduction;
- local orthonormal, selected-coordinate, slice-proper/ordinary-anchor, mutual-clock, one-way, and
  roundtrip diagnostics kept separate;
- static zero-shift path integration with arbitrary positive spatial/angular path weights;
- both reciprocal wings, all power-law threading classes `F~D^alpha`, mixed-regime connectors, and
  a nontrivial adjacency family `L_AB=lambda*sigma`;
- flat noncentral reciprocal plateaus and one exact positive smooth planar transition family with
  free width;
- current-source ownership of `phi`, `c_E`, `F`, CSN, observer reciprocity, co-presence, angular/
  shift assembly, scale, bootstrap, and information interpretation.

## Dropped or still open under the ten criteria

1. **Fields:** no complete off-shell metric/coframe/`phi` field content is selected.
2. **Action terms:** no action is used or selected.
3. **Full equations:** no realization operator or EOM exists in this audit.
4. **Domain/coordinates:** the exact general control is a supplied stationary connector; arbitrary
   time-live nonspherical 3+1 assembly remains open.
5. **Boundary/regularity:** a planar transition family is classified; complete finite-cell seal,
   cap, seam, boundary functional, and global transition remain open.
6. **Topology:** no topology or quotient is selected.
7. **Dynamics:** metric-causal relations are classified; no physical time evolution or material
   propagation law is supplied.
8. **Branches:** all algebraic `F` power classes and named path/shift controls are retained, but this
   is not a census of every arbitrary function or global metric.
9. **Stability:** not applicable to this kinematic tile; no matter configuration is evolved.
10. **Regime of validity:** exact for the stated metric controls and formal asymptotic families.
    Physical microscopic/cosmological placement and an absolute `sigma` remain open.

Every dropped item could host emergent structure. None is converted into a negative about the full
metric. The computation uses only exact algebra on existing metric forms; no physics mechanism,
linearization, fitting, cutoff, carrier, or desired-solution filter is introduced.
