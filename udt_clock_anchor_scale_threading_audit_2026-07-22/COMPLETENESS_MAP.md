## Characterized but not universally promoted

- co-presence without a foundational carrier: compatible working ontology, not a response theorem;
- `F`: contains genuine radial geometry, not selected by current premises;
- outer clock freeze: conditional combined asymptotic class;
- inner reflection: singular at a regular spherical areal center, open elsewhere;
- ordinary `X~F~1`: intended observational calibration, not a derived tolerance/profile; and
- `R->0`: an areal-center limit, not automatically physical subnuclear separation.

## Not covered

- full time-dependent curvature for every `F(t,R),X(t,R)` branch;
- global synchronization, topology, quotient, seam, or holonomy selection;
- action, EOM, source, carrier, material clock/signal, detector response, or no-signalling theorem;
- native mass, density, boundary generator, absolute `X_max`, or scale breaking;
- physical map from dimensionless geometry to terrestrial, cosmological, or micro regimes;
- empirical/canonical verdict.

## Ten-criterion placement

This package is one kinematic/curvature tile. It covers metric degrees `F,X` in a declared spherical
chart, local/static endpoint regularity, and regime classification. It does not cover a selected
action, full equations, matter fields, complete boundary data, topology, dynamics, branches of a
native EOM, stability, or physical scale. Those blank criteria can host emergent structure and
remain explicit blind spots.
