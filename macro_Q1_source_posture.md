## 0. The question

The macro background is a redshift profile `φ(r)` (plus transverse size `D_A(r)` / free `h_AB`). Something must make `φ` vary with position. Without variation: no redshift, no cosmology.

Native action (claimed, load-bearing): matter does **not** source `φ` **directly** (`δS_m/δφ = 0` to leading order, conditional on R1 shift levers). Matter reaches `φ` only **indirectly**, through geometry, and only when `𝒦` is **uncompensated** (P-type route). Compensated `𝒦` (G) is bulk-sourceless.

**Q1 = through which legal channel does the universe get a varying φ?**  
Past macro work assumed a smooth density, reverse-engineered `φ` from data, or borrowed a cell formula. None of those is a derived macro source.

## 2. Option cards (lay language)

### (a) Indirect geometric sourcing — PRIMARY

**Picture.** Matter deforms transverse geometry (`D_A` / `h_AB`). That feeds `𝒦`. With `𝒦` uncompensated, that feeds `φ`. Redshift is geometry-mediated, not a hand-added `ρ→φ` term.

**Assumes.** No direct `φ` source; matter couples to `h`; `h` free (not frozen to `r²Ω`); switch to uncompensated route when matter is on (plausible, **not derived** as switch criterion); clean `L_m` (content = Q4, open).

**If true, looks like.** Coupled `(φ, D_A, matter)` with regular center; scale from matter (cures “c,G alone”); edge may match a vacuum-like limit; cosine possible as *emergence*, never as target; closure amount is the interesting prediction hope.

**Kills.** Matter never reaches `𝒦`/φ with free `h`; only solutions that re-import cell package; or foundational audit shows direct `δS_m/δφ ≠ 0`.

**Traps.** Calling this “Branch P” and loading winding/seals/frozen round; bare `ρ_bg = M/(4/3 π R³)` as if derived; freezing `h` for ease; tuning matter to SNe.

### (b) Ensemble / average of cells — PARKED

**Picture.** Macro `φ` is the coarse-grain of many cell exteriors, not a fundamental smooth fluid.

**Assumes.** Cells with non-cancelling exterior fields; a native averaging law (not imported FLRW dust); effective macro equations tagged as effective.

**If true, looks like.** Source terms as moments of a cell distribution; dim-baryon story may connect later; ladder/cosine might reappear as statistics.

**Kills.** Averaging → zero net source; no controlled average without smuggling; “ensemble” used only to license arbitrary `ρ`.

**Why parked.** Defers Q1 to unfinished micro + averaging; fights the pivot (macro first so particles have a real background). Revisit after (a) is characterized.

---

### (c) Boundary data alone (G bulk) — REFERENCE / LIMIT

**Picture.** Bulk `φ` equation sourceless; profile set by charge `q` and boundaries (Coulomb / vacuum family).

**Assumes.** Compensated `𝒦` for macro bulk; redshift from `q ≠ 0` without regular-center contradiction — already in structural tension.

**Status.** Vacuum free-`D_A` G system: regular center + nontrivial redshift + `d_L → 0` as `z → 0` fail together; ~2 mag under both n=1 and n=2. Scale from pure boundary without mass: dimensional tension.

**Role under posture.** Mathematical branch of the action retained as **edge / vacuum-as-limit reference**, not as the full macro answer. Do not re-run “macro = Branch G” as the program.
