# Lay Decision Tree

The earlier search had 51 starting points where the numerical method got stuck.  That did **not**
mean 51 new universes or 51 impossible solutions; it meant the route finder had stopped.

We replaced the direct route with a winding numerical road that can travel around bends and folds.

- 29 formerly stuck routes now reach the same round geometry as the easy routes.
- 22 routes are still on the winding road when their registered clock expires.
- none reaches a different geometry.

So the round geometry has become a much stronger local picture, but we still cannot say it is the
only possible geometry.  The remaining routes, larger function spaces, physical edge of the cell,
time-dependent universe, matter, and scale are still open.

The bends found here belong to the route-finding map.  They are not time evolution and are not a new
physical force.
