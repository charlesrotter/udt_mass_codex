## What is retained

and second-jet configurations of a conditional four-dimensional metric, together with conditional
split bookkeeping and local positive common-scale equivalence.

Its load-bearing exact statements remain:

- 15 real symmetric four-dimensional zero-jet inertia classes;
- 36 conditional base/screen inertia products;
- 8 `dphi` support/causal witnesses;
- 12 conditional expansion/shear/twist rank witnesses;
- 7 full-curvature rank witnesses, 5 Ricci-rank witnesses, and 6 algebraic invariant-sector types;
- the complete 20-dimensional algebraic-curvature space; and
- its unique 10-plus-10 decomposition into a common-scale-invariant sector and a
  representative-dependent sector.

The P02 main and independent checks remain valid and its package is byte-identical to the base.

## What is withdrawn from P02's interpretation

P02's reports sometimes described the local map by contrast with particular candidate actions,
equations, desired `phi` branches, preferred curvature directions, or a future "selector." That
framing is withdrawn as target-shaped. Those were not questions posed by the local atlas, and their
absence is not part of the P02 result.

The corrected neutral statement is:

> P02 mapped the complete registered point-local configuration space in its conditional metric
> arena and identified which of its recorded distinctions are unchanged or changed under local
> positive common-scale transformations. It supplied no dynamics question and selected no
> configuration.

P03 uses only that statement. It neither edits P02 nor treats P02 as evidence for or against any
particular dynamics.
