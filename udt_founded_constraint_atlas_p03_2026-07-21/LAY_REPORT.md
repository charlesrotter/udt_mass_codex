# P03 in plain language

We stopped asking whether the metric produces any particular answer and instead asked a bookkeeping
question: what does each accepted UDT premise actually change in the full local map we already made?

Three different jobs had been getting blurred together.

First, Reciprocity acts on the abstract clock/ruler comparison. It says the two channels compensate:
once one is known, the other is its reciprocal. That turns two positive channel strengths into one
relative degree of freedom. But UDT has not yet supplied the missing connection that tells this
abstract pair which two directions of a general spacetime metric it controls.

Second, Common-Scale Neutrality says that two local metric descriptions differing only by a shared
change of scale are the same pre-material geometry. This groups descriptions together; it does not
discard a geometry. In the curvature map, 20 local curvature numbers split into 10 that survive this
identification and 10 that describe the chosen scale representative. In a supplied angular split,
the apparent expansion changes with the common scale, while shear and twist remain.

Third, the finite-cell seal supplies one genuine but narrowly located restriction: in the static
`phi` branch, `phi` and its allowed parity-preserving variation vanish at the seal. Its normal slope
is still free. The broader finite-cell and bootstrap ideas are global requirements and currently add
no equation to a point inside the cell.

The result is that all 89 local witnesses from P02 remain represented in the current overall
foundation. One conventional Lorentzian row and one reciprocal-split row describe familiar supplied
branches, but those choices are still conditional. No current premise universally selects a
spacetime reciprocal plane, a local `phi` gradient class, a twist class, a curvature type, or a
physical common-scale representative.

That is not a failed search. It is an objective map of where the present foundation acts and where it
does not. The kinematic comparison law is constrained; the local metric is organized into
equivalence classes; the seal has one scalar condition; the remaining local-to-global and
kinematics-to-metric joins are still unfilled.
