## Abstract comparison branch

```text

```

The regular additive character can be written, after a chosen sign/unit convention for the signed
comparison argument `phi`, as

```text

D(a+b) = D(a)D(b),
det D(phi) = 1.
```

This is an internal comparison relation. There is no map from this pair to a spacetime plane in the
foundation used by P03. Accordingly it reduces two positive channel weights to one relative
character but imposes zero equations on the P02 metric jets until a separate soldering is supplied.

corner, or boundary-functional condition is inferred.
