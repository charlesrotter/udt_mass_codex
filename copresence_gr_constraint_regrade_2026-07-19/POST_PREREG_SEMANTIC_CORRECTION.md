# Post-Preregistration Semantic Correction

The preregistered question, candidate classes, outcome set, maximum conclusion, and stop boundary are
unchanged. Two initial adversarial reviews rejected the first draft because it still forced several
non-constraint questions into constraint roles and its semantic verifier did not bind all fields.

The correction is conservative and fail-closed:

- the eight preregistered constraint distinctions remain present;
- the taxonomy adds explicit non-constraint roles for soft penalties, symmetry and domain premises,
  field census, CSN/scaling, calibration, selection maps, existence witnesses, declared-field
  variation rules, and parent-law/action-bridge dependencies;
- hard substitution is restored separately with its exact prior `REFUTED_GENERICALLY` status;
- CSN is split into `FOUNDING`, derived positive-rescaling causal invariance, and open physical
  clock/ruler calibration;
- `P07` is narrowed from an overbroad post-scale lapse/shift comparison to
  `COMPARISON_FORMALISM_ONLY_NO_SHIFT_ANCHOR`, because the executable derives a lapse-like toy but no
  shift-like term;
- Hamiltonian- and momentum-like projections require a parent equation and foliation, while their
  promotion to initial-data constraints additionally requires principal-part, data, and propagation
  analysis;
- every thematic record now carries source, scientific status, operator provenance, prerequisites,
  co-presence effect, overlay disposition, remaining scope, and future gate;
- a 37-row crosswalk preserves controlling source conclusions and exact source statuses;
- the verifier binds the complete semantic tables and report, replays all 36 entries in the three
  cited source manifests, and exercises the new category-error catches.

No frozen source was changed. No GR constraint, shift ontology, field census, action, boundary
completion, or bridge was adopted by this correction.
