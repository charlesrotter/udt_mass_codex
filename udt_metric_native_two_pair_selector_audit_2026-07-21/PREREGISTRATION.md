Date: 2026-07-21

## Frozen question

Does the already registered UDT metric structure—Common-Scale Neutrality (`CSN`), the founding
clock/ruler reciprocal pair, the complete finite-cell seal lifts, and metric-native angular
geometry—derive a normalized second reciprocal pair on the physical four-dimensional conformal
frame bundle? Or does the full two-pair structure found by the parent audit remain conditional?

This is a selector audit. It may not select a construction because it resembles the conditional
Hopf carrier or repairs an unwanted result.

  exists; the direct transverse-identity realization is generically angularly obstructed, while a

## Required selector properties

A successful complete two-pair selector must provide, on its declared physical domain:

1. a real normalized endomorphism `L` with `L^2=I`, two `+1` and two `-1` directions, and the
   founding longitudinal reciprocal action;
2. a metric-compatible/self-adjoint or separately metric-derived interpretation;
3. the required seal conjugation `R L R=-L`;
4. CSN covariance with no representative-scale dependence;
5. uniqueness up to explicitly harmless global discrete exchange, not a continuously chosen axis;
6. existence on the trivial and isotropic branches or a UDT-derived reason those branches are
   excluded;
7. continuation through eigenvalue/gradient degeneracies without an inserted framing;
8. global bulk definition, not merely pointwise boundary data at the seal.

## Preregistered falsifiers

The result is **not derived** if any load-bearing candidate:

- selects only a `1+3` or longitudinal/transverse `2+2` split but not a reciprocal pair within the
  angular plane;
- fails when `d phi=0`, curvature vanishes, or a transverse eigenvalue repeats;
- has an unresolved sign, axis, ordering, or continuous stabilizer ambiguity that changes `L`;
- is defined only at the seal and requires an unselected connection to enter the bulk;
- relies on the Hopf carrier, spin lift, angular periodicity, cap, or desired soliton as input;
- is conformal-representative dependent;
- mistakes uniqueness conditional on existence for existence or physical selection; or
- turns bootstrap admissibility into a missing local differential law.

6. source/status review separating metric authority, conditional spacetime realization, and
   carrier diagnostics; and
