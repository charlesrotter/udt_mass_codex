1. **Finite reach** — like a sibling of \(c\): there is a maximum compositional distance \(X\); depth adds; you never cross the bound.

\[

\quad
\lim_{x\to X}m=\frac{X}{2},

**Critical closure is automatic** at the reach bound under J1.
