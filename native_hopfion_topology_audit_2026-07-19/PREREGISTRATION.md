## Whole question

Does the already-committed UDT field content and finite-cell boundary structure, without adding a
field, action term, coupling, source, or structure-holding boundary condition, naturally define a
nontrivial three-dimensional Hopf sector? Equivalently, does the existing theory supply both:

1. a compactified spatial domain equivalent to `S^3`; and
2. a physical state map into `S^2` whose Hopf invariant is well defined?

The audit separately asks whether the present static particle ansatz or numerical representation
could observe a nonzero Hopf invariant. It does not target a Hopfion solution.

## Why this is metric-led

The primary question is what topology is already present in the registered metric, fields, carrier
premises, and boundary data. Standard Hopf and homotopy constructions are category-A mathematical
diagnostics only. They may classify an existing UDT map; they may not supply missing physics.

## Registered arms

### Arm M — metric/foundation-native

Audit whether the exact foundational metric/state variables and their registered equivalences define
a physical `S^2` target and whether the native finite-cell boundary completes a spatial slice to
`S^3`. This arm fails closed if either ingredient is supplied only by the carrier posit or by an
imported model.

### Arm C — existing carrier-conditional

Condition on exactly the already-registered `S^2` carrier posit and its actual particle boundary
conditions. Determine whether a continuous map `S^3 -> S^2` is mathematically available and whether
the saved/static solution representation contains enough genuinely three-dimensional degrees of
freedom to carry or measure a nonzero Hopf invariant.

### Arm A — ansatz/representation exclusion

Audit whether radial, hedgehog, symmetry-reduced, component-frozen, or boundary-pinned choices make a
nonzero Hopf invariant impossible or unobservable. A representation-level exclusion is not a no-go
for the unreduced UDT field space.

## Certification and falsification contract

A `NATIVE_HOPF_SECTOR_DERIVED` conclusion is permitted only if current UDT sources independently
supply all of: physical domain, boundary quotient, physical target, continuous map, gauge/equivalence
handling, and an integer invariant. No carrier posit or imported action may bear any of those loads.

A `CARRIER_CONDITIONAL_HOPF_SECTOR_AVAILABLE` conclusion is permitted only if the exact existing
carrier and boundary premises supply a well-defined `S^3 -> S^2` map. This does not establish a
nonzero existing solution, stability, or carrier emergence.

An `EXISTING_CONFIGURATION_NONZERO_HOPF_CHARGE` conclusion is permitted only if a loadable existing
three-dimensional configuration supports an independently recomputed, converged integer charge.
The filename, a `Q=1` label, energy, or a Skyrme degree is not evidence of Hopf charge.

An `ANSATZ_EXCLUDES_NONZERO_HOPF_CHARGE` conclusion is permitted only with an analytic factorization
or symmetry argument plus a runnable catch checked on a known nonzero Hopf map. It remains scoped to
that ansatz/representation.

If the domain is not compactified, the target is not physical `S^2`, the boundary is nonconstant, the
state is not reconstructable in 3D, or the invariant depends on an invented projection, the relevant
conclusion is `OPEN`, `UNAVAILABLE_IN_REGISTERED_FRAME`, or `UNDERDETERMINED`, not a negative on UDT.

## Runnable checks and independence

Any algebra/code added will:

- implement a canonical nonzero Hopf map as a positive control and constant/radial maps as zero
  controls;
- distinguish a Skyrme degree `S^3 -> S^3` from a Hopf invariant `S^3 -> S^2`;
- fail if a carrier-conditional result is relabeled native;
- fail if nonconstant boundary data are silently compactified to one point;
- fail if a symmetry-reduced representation is claimed to exhaust the full 3D field space;
- record exact CPU/Python/dependency versions and raw output hashes.

Before banking, a fresh adversarial context must independently inspect the load-bearing sources and
rerun or rederive the decisive topology claim. Catch-proofs must be exercised, not merely asserted.

## Completeness map and stop conditions

This audit covers field/target provenance, spatial domain, boundary compactification, topological
sector availability, and ansatz observability. It does not cover a complete action, full equations,
time evolution, branch census, stability spectrum, mass, cosmology, or carrier emergence.

Stop after a verified classification package. Do not launch a 3D relaxation, time-live solve, GPU
work, action search, parameter fit, canonization, repository reorganization, or navigation update.

## Maximum allowed conclusion

The maximum return is a premise-scoped classification of whether Hopf topology is native,
carrier-conditional, merely mathematically possible, or excluded by the existing ansatz, together
with the smallest next falsifiable study. This audit cannot establish a Hopfion particle, native
matter, stable time evolution, a complete UDT action, or canon.
