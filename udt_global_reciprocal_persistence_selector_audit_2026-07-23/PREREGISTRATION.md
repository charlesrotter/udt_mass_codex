## Exact controls and counterconfigurations

```text
phi_linear(x)=x
phi_critical(x)=x^3
phi_trivial(x)=0.
```

well-defined reciprocal character `D(phi)`, and neither changes the founding
group law. But:


the cubic profile has a critical point at the seal, and the trivial profile
has no derivative-based split anywhere.

be on-shell matter-bearing universes and cannot be used to rank their
physical merit.
