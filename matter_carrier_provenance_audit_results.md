## 1. The binary answer

**NO — the S² matter carrier does not trace back to the metric. It is a genuine additional posit.**
The two bracketing canon entries make the seam exact:

- **Metric side (C-2026-06-18-1) leaves the slot empty.** Relativity derives the metric's *form* and then
  *"leaves FREE: the angular/transverse block, all off-diagonal/shift terms, the time-dependence of φ, the
  chart, and the topology."* The metric derivation forces the dilation structure and hands you an **empty
  angular slot** — it does not force any matter field.
- **Matter side (C-2026-06-14-1) fills that slot from a different source.** The carrier is *"UDT's own
  angular-sector field — the unit 3-vector n_a … the same object carrying N=3, q=1/3,"* whose provenance
  (CANON:160) traces to *"the original dpf density"* — the **angular sector, born from the integer/digit-search
  insight**, not from solving the metric. F2 took it as given and said so (F2-1: *"target S² — DERIVED (canon)
  — itself an upstream posit, not re-opened"*).

## 3. What it clarifies (the result, not a failure)

1. **UDT derives the stage, not the actors.** UDT = (derived dilation metric with a free angular slot) +
   (a *posited* angular-sector matter field that fills it). The orchestra metaphor is the literal structure:
   the metric builds the hall and the empty seats; the integer insight supplies the instruments.
2. **Native-motivated posit, not an SM import.** It is UDT's own integer-insight object (N=3, q=1/3), and the
   action on it is genuinely metric-built — so "not derived from the metric" ≠ "smuggled from the Standard
   Model." But relative to the "derive from the metric" standard, it **is** an additional postulate. UDT is a
   clean **two-postulate** theory (dilation metric + one matter postulate), not a one-postulate theory the
   word "native" was starting to imply.
3. **The honest resolution of the static-mass debate.** **Matter-sector properties (mass included) can be
   NATIVE TO THE ACTION without being DERIVABLE FROM THE METRIC.** The mass kept refusing to close cleanly
   because we were trying to derive a property of the actors from the stage, and the actors were never in the
   stage. That is the work correctly refusing to manufacture a bridge that is not there — not a failure.
4. **It gates the downstream questions.** Since the carrier is posited: the coupled localization solver would
   prove a *posited* object localizes (legitimate, but not deriving matter from the metric); and joining the
   hopfion's smooth core to the metric's singular seat would *posit* an identification, not derive one.
