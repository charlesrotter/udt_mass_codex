## Whole question

Do the existing UDT metric records describe one derived asymptotic boundary, or several distinct
objects that acquired similar names? In particular, determine the exact lineage and possible joins
among:

- the finite-cell/CMB fold;
- the simple-metric WR-L endpoint `X` where `A=1-r/X` tends to zero;
- chart or optical endpoints;
- any finite-cell cap or seal; and
- the currently defined global output `X_max`.

For each record, extract what is actually asymptotic in the clock, ruler, proper radial distance,
proper volume, optical distance, curvature, and mass channels. Distinguish equations from
interpretation and determine whether “mass dilation” was derived, conditionally read through an
imported action/source, or only stated.

This is **metric-led**: it asks what the recorded metric and its exact invariants already do. It is
not a search for a desired cosmology, a GR horizon, or a mass formula.

## Falsification and certification contract

The proposed single-boundary join fails if any load-bearing equality depends only on reused
notation, incompatible `phi` normalizations, different metric branches, an input `X`, an imported
mass definition, or an unproved action/source. It also fails if a finite proper-distance result is
mistaken for finite optical reach, or if regular curvature is mistaken for a field equation.

A join may be graded no higher than `UNIQUE_CONDITIONAL` unless the same derived scalar surface is
identified without a supplied `X`, with compatible charts/branches, and with every required metric
quantity transformed explicitly. Mass closure additionally requires a native mass structure; this
audit cannot manufacture one.

Certification requires:

1. deterministic candidate and claim ledgers;
2. rerun of every load-bearing saved algebra calculation that is available and CPU-bounded;
3. an independent implementation of the decisive limits;
4. exercised catch-proofs for notation-only joins, `phi` convention swaps, input/output circularity,
   imported mass promotion, and proper-versus-optical distance confusion;
5. an adversarial fresh-context review before banking;
6. replay of package/frozen manifests, links/frontier targets, tests, and dirty-checkout metadata.

## Completeness and maximum conclusion

not cover every metric solution, time-live global closure, native matter emergence, or a complete
action. The dropped fields, actions, sources, angular sectors, branches, and time-live solutions are

distinct, conditional, or open, followed by a noncircular equation/unknown ledger for later work.
No numerical `X_max`, total mass, native source, action, carrier, canonization, GPU solve, or new
repository reorganization is authorized.
