PREMISE LEDGER:

BREAKER: the single-cell breathing tower (already a cavity-mode attempt) FAILED

### B. WHERE IS THE QUANTUM "i"? — the dear half

is REAL and P/T-even — which is EXACTLY why every Berry phase computed came back
zero (crux2). A real medium gives real standing waves, not a complex phase. For
quantization to emerge, the metric must keep an "i" somewhere. Two native candidates:

B1. THE NATIVE U(1) / PHOTON carries the phase.
CLAIM: UDT forced a native U(1) — the photon (#47). The U(1) phase IS the quantum
phase (the same circle). Quantizing a charged texture = winding that native U(1) as
it moves through the dilation medium; interference is electromagnetic in origin.
EXPLAINS: the intimacy of charge and quantum phase; why a neutral object is the
harder thing to quantize.
PREMISE: native U(1) is DERIVED (#47); "U(1) phase = QM phase" is CHOSEN.
BREAKER (strong): NEUTRAL particles still interfere (the neutron). If the i lived
ONLY in the EM U(1), neutral matter would not be quantum — but it is. So B1 alone
cannot be the whole story; at most it is the charged sector's share of the phase.

B2. CLOSED TIME supplies the i AND the double cover.
CLAIM: if canon time is a CIRCLE (not an open line), wavefunctions on it come in two
kinds — periodic and ANTIperiodic; the antiperiodic sector IS the double cover (the
spinor). The electron's two-valuedness = a wave on a temporal ring.

target; ties the i AND the spinor to ONE structure — time's topology.
PREMISE: "time closes into a circle" is CANON-CANDIDATE, NOT settled (STATE open

"CONDITIONS-CHANGED if time closes." B2 is precisely that reopener.
BREAKER: if time is genuinely open, B2 dies. If closed, one must still show the
antiperiodic sector is SELECTED / physical, not merely admitted.

### C. hbar IS THE THIRD DOMINO — the action of one turn of a geometric clock

CLAIM: UDT turned c into geometry, then mass into geometry; hbar is the third stone
in that wall. Following de Broglie: every particle carries an internal clock (the

COROLLARY (dissolves the spin wall): if the internal clock is a CIRCULATION (a
zitterbewegung-like whirl), spin-1/2 / the double cover come WITH it — a spinning

EXPLAINS: hbar itself (not imported); the unity of mass, clock, and spin; why
canonical quantization (which imports hbar) could never see this.
PREMISE LEDGER:

BREAKER (deepest, make-or-break for the whole emergent programme): if hbar CANNOT be
written as a metric ratio, the third domino fails and "emergent quantization"
