# Next scientific decision

The product control rules out intrinsic scalar curvature by itself as the local selector. It also
shows that another fixed-`K` profile search would merely explore a family already known not to be
selected.

The smallest next metric-led audit is therefore:

> Construct a gauge-invariant reciprocal 2+2 coframe with transverse area, shear, and the complete
> screen/twist connection retained; derive its conditional `C2` equations and endpoint currents;
> determine whether those equations select the product-family constants or instead defer selection
> to the global finite-cell boundary/bootstrap.

Required stop conditions:

- no carrier, section, topology, cap, or boundary value may be chosen to make the equations close;
- the product metric in this package must be recovered as an exact control limit;
- no reduced equation may replace the complete metric variation;
- every directional connection term must be kept or shown to be gauge;
- a local negative may not be promoted to a global bootstrap verdict.

CPU symbolic work is the appropriate first stage. GPU solution-space exploration is premature until
the full coframe equations and admissible boundary data are defined.
