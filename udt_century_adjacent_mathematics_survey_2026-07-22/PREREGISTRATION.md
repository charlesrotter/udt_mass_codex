## Whole question

Across approximately the last century of geometry, topology, global analysis, variational theory,
mathematical relativity, and soliton mathematics, what established structures, theorems,
obstructions, and counterexamples help us recognize what the registered UDT metric already
contains—and what mathematical datum it genuinely lacks—without importing any external action,
field equation, matter model, carrier, boundary law, scale mechanism, or physical interpretation?

The active UDT seam is the distinction between **admissibility** and **realization**. The survey must
also cover the typed two-form/holonomy and cocycle/seam questions identified by the frozen
metric-to-frontier review. It may sharpen a UDT question; it may not answer that question with an
external theory.

## Observing or targeting

`OBSERVING_AND_CLASSIFYING`. The survey does not target GR, conformal gravity, a Hopf carrier, an
action, a mass formula, or a preferred topology. It asks what each external result actually needs
as input, whether UDT currently supplies those hypotheses, and what remains missing.

## Time and source scope

- Main window: 1925–2026. An older antecedent may appear only if a post-1925 primary source cannot
  be understood without it; such rows are segregated as `ANTECEDENT_ONLY`.
- Minimum depth target: 36 primary research sources, at least three distinct decades in each of the
  four major blocks below, and at least one limitation/counterexample source wherever the literature
  contains one.
- Admitted sources: original research papers, authors' archival manuscripts, journal or proceedings
  versions, and official scholarly repositories. Later review papers may route discovery but may
  not carry a load-bearing theorem unless the original is unavailable and that limitation is explicit.
- No popular account, encyclopedia, blog, or unsourced historical summary can carry a scientific row.
- Source metadata and claims are frozen only after the primary page or paper is opened and checked.

## Frozen family map before search

### Block A — what a metric canonically supplies

1. natural differential operators, concomitants, and equivalence problems;
2. holonomy, de Rham decomposition, `G`-structures, and curvature-generated reductions;
3. conformal/projective structures, representative selection, and scale covariance;
4. exterior algebra, Hodge/curvature operators, characteristic classes, and twisted orientation.

### Block B — local data to complete global geometry

5. bundles, transition cocycles, monodromy, characteristic obstructions, caps, seams, and gluing;
6. boundary-value, elliptic/hyperbolic/constraint, differentiability, and boundary-generator data;
7. spectral geometry, inverse spectral limits, and homothetic scaling.

### Block C — admissibility versus realization

8. inverse variational problem, Helmholtz conditions, variational bicomplex, and symmetric-criticality limits;
9. fixed points, nonlinear eigenproblems, self-consistency maps, and existence-versus-selection;
10. geometric flows and constraint propagation as examples of extra evolution data, never as UDT laws.

### Block D — topology and stable structure controls

11. Hopf invariants, harmonic maps, topological solitons, Derrick scaling, and related stability
    mathematics, strictly as controls on what topology/action data do or do not accomplish.

No family may be silently dropped because it fails to resemble UDT. No family may be added after
outcomes merely because it supplies an attractive mechanism; additions require a committed
correction preregistration.

## Anti-import firewall

An external result may do only five jobs:

1. name a structure already constructed from UDT data;
2. supply universal mathematics that receives zero UDT-specific credit;
3. supply a theorem whose every hypothesis is independently verified in UDT before application;
4. provide a counterexample or obstruction to an inference;
5. formulate a bounded question to return to the UDT metric.

It may not:

- choose an action, equation, functional, fixed-point map, flow, connection, topology, carrier,
  source, boundary condition, charge, mass, density, or scale for UDT;
- turn formal resemblance into derivation or physical identity;
- use historical success or familiarity as evidence of UDT applicability;
- treat a theorem's conclusion as available when any hypothesis is absent or only analogous;
- use an external target value, normalization, spectral scale, or asymptotic condition to close UDT.

## Completeness and anti-imposition map

This is one comparative tile, not a survey of all twentieth-century mathematical physics. It covers
the eleven frozen families and drops detailed quantum field theory, Standard Model phenomenology,
statistical mechanics, condensed matter, numerical methods, and observational cosmology except
when needed to classify a forbidden import. Those omissions may contain useful analogies but cannot
presently close the registered metric-native seams without importing additional physics.

The survey characterizes literature relationships; it does not filter UDT solutions or rank a
branch by resemblance, elegance, popularity, or empirical success.

## Falsification and certification contract

The survey fails if:

1. any load-bearing row lacks a checked primary source and stable link;
2. a mathematical conclusion is applied without all hypotheses listed;
3. an analogy is promoted to a direct match;
4. an external action, field equation, carrier, boundary rule, scale, or target value is proposed as
   affirmative UDT content;
5. universal metric/Lorentzian structure is reported as UDT-specific emergence;
6. a famous framework is privileged while contrary or limitation results are omitted;
7. source counts, decades, family coverage, or row identities do not replay mechanically;
8. the frozen UDT reference, prior packages, tests, links/frontier, or dirty-checkout metadata change.

Fresh adversarial review must try to find citation misstatements, hypothesis gaps, analogy
promotions, and hidden imports. If no fresh external review is authorized, the maximum grade is
`VERIFIED_WITH_CAVEATS__NO_FRESH_EXTERNAL_REVIEW`.
