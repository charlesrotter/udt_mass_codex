# Decision after P04

P04 stops before equations.

The next registered stage is P05, but it should not merge the three lanes:

1. A bounded `P05-L01` package may derive the complete pre-scale `C^2`/Bach operator and variation
   graph across all compatible or blocked field-realization subbranches, including the full
   finite-cell boundary-current ledger. It must stop rather than invent equations for extra fields.
2. A separate bounded `P05-L02` package may derive the post-scale EH operator and variation graph
   under an explicitly supplied representative/scale placeholder, again branching across every
   field realization and finite-cell boundary variable. The placeholder is not emergence.
3. `L03` is not ready for P05. It first needs a mathematically specified selection/matching map; an
   action may not be fabricated merely to populate the lane.

Running `P05-L01` and `P05-L02` as parallel conditional operator builds would permit a later neutral
comparison of their complete premise footprints. Neither build may solve an equation, rank a lane,
or use empirical agreement as feedback.

A new explicit dispatch is required before either P05 build.
