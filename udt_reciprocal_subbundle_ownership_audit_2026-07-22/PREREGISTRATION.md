## Typed objects

The audit will keep four objects distinct:

1. `V_rec`: the abstract two-dimensional reciprocal representation carrying
   `D(phi)=diag(exp(-phi),exp(phi))`;
2. `E`: a rank-two subbundle/distribution of the complete tangent or coframe bundle;

An abstract representation does not by itself supply `E`, and a pointwise `E` does not by itself
supply `T`.

## Selection standard

A candidate counts as a complete intrinsic selector only if it:

1. is constructed from registered data without a labeled-slot or frame-gauge choice;
2. is defined on every admitted stratum, including `phi=0`, `d phi=0`, flat/round/repeated-spectrum
   points, and the static seal;
3. gives one rank-two physical subbundle up to a declared gauge equivalence;
4. has a registered or derived soldering to the abstract reciprocal pair;
5. is compatible with CSN and admissible internal-frame/chart changes;
6. extends through the finite cell without an inserted path, cover, connection, or boundary lift;
7. does not use an action, carrier, topology, empirical target, or desired solution as a filter.

An orbit of equally admissible candidates under a stabilizer does not count as a selected object:
an intrinsic selector must be fixed/equivariant under every automorphism of the supplied data.
