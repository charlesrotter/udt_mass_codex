# Completeness Map

Date: 2026-07-21

## Complete inside the registered box

- all 6,144 immutable metric/phi pointwise two-jets;
- all 31 nonempty subsets of `R`, `H`, `D`, `RG`, and `WG`;
- all 190,464 original family rows;
- all 75 directed one-instrument additions and 460,800 original edge rows;
- primitive central blocks, metric signatures, scalar-instrument discriminants, gradient incidence,
  Ricci/Hessian split alignment, and edge-transition labels;
- both preregistered nonlinear coordinate three-jet probes for every family;
- outcome-blind independent recomputation on 384 configurations and exhaustive independent replay of
  every unique identity in the 162-row numerical-margin ledger.

## Sampled but not group-exhaustive

- covariance: two nonlinear chart probes, not the full diffeomorphism group;
- numerical conditioning: the registered finite ensemble and all observed margins, not arbitrary
  jets approaching every algebraic discriminant surface.
- gradient causal class: zero, spacelike, and timelike gradients are present; nonzero null gradients
  are absent.

## Not present and therefore open

- metric or scalar jets above second order;
- variation, an action, field equations, source, or coupling law;
- time evolution, characteristic propagation, transport, or holonomy along a path;
- integrability of pointwise blocks into distributions or sections;
- global topology, finite-cell seal, boundary polarization, or boundary functional;
- physical scale, `c`, `G`, `X_max`, mass, density, or an observational anchor;
- a native carrier, matter emergence, unconditional stability, or mass;
- a unique physical branch, solution, universe, or observable prediction.

The atlas is exhaustive over its finite pointwise instrument lattice. It is not a solution-space
atlas for an action that has not been derived, and it supplies no permission to choose one motif as
physical.
