# Preregistration correction — open-path transport is not holonomy

Date: 2026-07-22

The committed preregistration says Tile A will record "permutation holonomy" along each 17-node
affine path. Those paths are open, not closed. They can record projector continuation, matching,
and endpoint permutation transport, but cannot define holonomy.

Before any new motif-to-Hopf outcome is computed, replace that requested readout operationally by:

```text
projector continuation and endpoint permutation transport along an open path;
holonomy status = NOT_TESTED_BY_OPEN_PATH.
```

No artificial endpoint identification or closed loop may be added. Any future holonomy calculation
requires a separately declared closed curve and connection. All other preregistered coverage,
thresholds, gates, and maximum conclusions remain unchanged.
