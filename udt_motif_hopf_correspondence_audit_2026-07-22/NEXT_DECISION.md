# Next decision

No continuation is authorized by this package.

The evidence does not recommend another larger pointwise motif scan. The most informative remaining
question is global assembly:

> Across the admissible finite-cell completion classes, which sampled local projector motifs extend
> globally, what transition/cap data do they carry, and does any metric/bootstrap condition select
> the reciprocal-toric commuting stratum and its circle action without importing the Hopf carrier?

A future phase should first define and verify continuous projector transport rather than assume it,
then remain a geometry atlas rather than target `Q=1`. It should retain open,
periodic, same-cycle, opposite-cycle, lens, singular, nonorientable, and nonintegrable completions;
compute their intrinsic bundle characteristic data; and report every branch. Only a globally
selected metric quotient would justify attempting to derive a reduced action and comparing its
stationary sector with the existing conditional soliton.

Time-live carrier simulation, `L2+L4` derivation, GPU work, action selection, source/mass work, and
canonization remain later and separately gated.
