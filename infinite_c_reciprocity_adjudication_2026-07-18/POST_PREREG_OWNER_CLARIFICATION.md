# Post-preregistration owner clarification

Date received: 2026-07-18  
Status: owner historical/conceptual input received after preregistration; audited, not automatically adopted

The owner clarified that the motivating picture predates the current formal derivation by roughly
35 years:

> “I also viewed c as a observational anchor of the geometric universe with the underlying
> foundational speed being infinite. I'd look at stars and imagine electrons swapping energy states
> instantly as the cause of the distant light, with the time of travel being a consequence of warped
> space time.”

This is not treated as H1's claim that one and the same geometric conversion is simultaneously
finite and infinite. Its strongest coherent reading is a layered candidate:

```text
H5_LAYERED_ATEMPORAL_TO_OBSERVATIONAL_GEOMETRY
```

- the underlying foundation has no observable propagation delay—or perhaps no primitive geometric
  time in which a speed is defined;
- `c_obs` is a finite universal observational anchor of the emergent geometric universe;
- `1/c_obs` is geometric slowness converting spatial separation/path into observational travel time;
- observable causality is governed by the emergent metric, not by an accessible infinite-speed
  signalling channel.

Calling the underlying operation “infinite speed” may be shorthand. If spacetime time and distance
are emergent, literal speed is not yet a well-typed pregeometric quantity; “atemporal/global closure”
is the safer candidate until a substrate clock and distance are supplied.

This interpretation is conceptually compatible with retaining Reciprocal-c and dual Reciprocity at
the observational-geometric layer. It does not derive them. It adds four major obligations:

1. a native map/bootstrap from the atemporal/global layer to one Lorentz conformal geometry;
2. universal coupling of matter/readouts to that single cone;
3. a no-observable-superluminal-signalling theorem despite the underlying global relation;
4. finite-cell/global selection of the finite baseline slowness and its dimensionless observables.

An electron transition must be distinguished from an observable signal. A local or globally
constrained state change may be treated as atemporal in the candidate ontology, but the observer's
access to it must remain restricted by the emergent null geometry. Standard finite transition
durations or other empirical details are not adjudicated here.

The clarification does not authorize changes to C0/C1, canon, the current founding hierarchy, or
any action. It is recorded transparently because it changes the strongest counterfactual from a
same-layer literal infinity to a layered emergence proposal.
