## Verdict: REFUTED

The synchronization fails in two material ways: Stage I contains a prior negative action-selection conclusion, and candidate/open premises are promoted to frozen founding status without an owner-adoption record among the twelve supplied files.

### 1. Historical action conclusion leaked into Stage I

The controller says Stage I receives no prior action conclusions ([UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_DERIVATION_DISPATCH.md](/tmp/udt_native_sync_audit.8UHf9X/UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_DERIVATION_DISPATCH.md:21)) and no earlier UDT action result ([same file](/tmp/udt_native_sync_audit.8UHf9X/UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_DERIVATION_DISPATCH.md:140)). The cold packet likewise claims to exclude every historical action-selection result ([UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_COLD_PACKET.md](/tmp/udt_native_sync_audit.8UHf9X/UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_COLD_PACKET.md:224)).

Nevertheless, Stage I is told that the combined founding ledger and kinematics do not determine an action, measure, locality class, or derivative order ([UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_COLD_PACKET.md](/tmp/udt_native_sync_audit.8UHf9X/UDT_NATIVE_ACTION_COLD_PACKET.md:118)). That reproduces the historical negative action conclusion in [UDT_RECIPROCAL_C_FOUNDING_POSTULATE_DERIVATION_RESULTS.md](/tmp/udt_native_sync_audit.8UHf9X/UDT_RECIPROCAL_C_FOUNDING_POSTULATE_DERIVATION_RESULTS.md:242), especially its counterfamily conclusion at lines 260–284.

This is not harmless framing: the historical counterfamily predates the owner-locked Common-Scale Neutrality requirement and is not shown there to respect it. It therefore cannot establish underdetermination for the newly synchronized ledger without a fresh CSN-compatible countermodel.

Minimal fix: replace cold-packet lines 118–127 with neutral scope language:

> “The supplied owner locks do not themselves specify the following items. Stage I must determine whether the combined ledger constrains or selects them, subject to the countermodel gate.”

The per-postulate statement at lines 71–73 is acceptable because it reports the explicit limited scope of Common-Scale Neutrality itself.

The exact \(S^2\) static functional at cold-packet lines 149–175 is historical material, but it is clearly labeled a conditional static survivor rather than a spacetime action. I do not count that as a leaked gravitational-action conclusion.
