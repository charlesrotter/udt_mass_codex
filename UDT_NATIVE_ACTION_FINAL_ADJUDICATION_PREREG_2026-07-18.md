## 2. July 1, 2026 provenance firewall

Material whose scientific content predates 2026-07-01 may be used only to identify a failure,
counterexample, circularity, import, superseded route, or question to test. It may not supply
affirmative UDT physics.

Affirmative entries in the final ledger must instead rest on one or more of:

1. the synchronized 2026-07-17 C0/C1 owner ledger;
2. the independent 2026-07-18 A/B derivations, after Arm-C challenge;
3. the 2026-07-18 Arm-C audit, after local mechanical rerun; or
4. a fresh algebraic implication encoded and rerun in the final package.

A post-firewall date does not itself prove a claim. All premise, variation-domain, boundary,
carrier, regime, and completeness stamps remain binding. A current packet that cites older material
cannot launder that material into affirmative authority.
