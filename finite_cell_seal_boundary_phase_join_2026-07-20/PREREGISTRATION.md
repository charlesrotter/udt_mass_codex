## Join sufficiency test

A source family counts as a **complete executable polarization join** only if, without an inserted
condition or completion, it supplies all of:

1. the boundary hypersurface/sector and its causal character;
2. which components of `delta h` are fixed, free, or constrained;
3. which components of `delta K` are fixed, free, or constrained;
4. any natural equations for `E` and `Pi_h` induced by the allowed variations;
5. the corner variation/data rule;
6. compatibility with CSN, reciprocity, and every affected field sector;
7. an off-shell status: the rule governs allowed variations before solving, rather than filtering
   completed solutions afterward;
8. no conflict with the explicit statement that `phi'` is free at the static seal.

A partial mapping must be recorded as partial even if it resembles a familiar Dirichlet, Neumann,
reflecting, horizon, or regularity condition.
