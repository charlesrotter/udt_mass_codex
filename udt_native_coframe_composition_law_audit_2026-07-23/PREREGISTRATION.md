## Mathematical-type audit

For each registered UDT premise, the audit will record whether it supplies:

1. a transformation acting on one configuration;
2. an equivalence relation on representatives;
3. a compatibility/gluing condition between supplied charts;
4. an on-shell filter on complete solutions; or
5. an actual multi-input composition operation.

These categories will not be conflated.

## Candidate requirements

A proposed native composition law will be tested against:

- **R1 — domain and output:** maps identified complete coframe/scalar inputs to
  a complete nondegenerate coframe/scalar output;
- **R2 — vertex recovery:** returns each input at its simplex vertex;
- **R3 — relabeling naturality:** relabeling inputs and weights relabels the
  same result;
- **R4 — common covariance:** a common allowed chart/frame transformation of
  all inputs commutes with composition;
- **R5 — representative independence:** physically equivalent independent
  coframe representatives cannot change the physical composed result unless
  the theory itself supplies their relative identification;
- **R6 — CSN compatibility:** common-scale equivalence is respected without
  inventing separate scale representatives;
- **R7 — Reciprocity compatibility:** the registered reciprocal action is
  intertwined rather than imposed after composition;
- **R8 — face/edge consistency:** restricting a zero weight agrees with the
  lower-dimensional rule;
- **R9 — scalar integrity:** any supplied `dphi` is the differential of a
  scalar in the claimed spacetime scope;
- **R10 — finite-cell compatibility:** any claimed seal consequence follows
  from the registered seal map and does not assume a missing lift;
- **R11 — operational bootstrap selection:** a claimed bootstrap selector is
  an explicit noncircular map/equation, not a verbal after-solution filter.

Meeting only a subset may qualify an operation as a useful coordinate chart,
but not as a native physical law.

## Preregistered falsification and certification contract

The hypothesis “the current registered UDT premises select a native complete-
coframe composition law” is certified only if an explicit operation is traced
to those premises and passes R1–R11 without adding an unregistered connection,
representative identification, distance, action, measure, or target outcome.

It is falsified at the present-premise level if:

1. no registered premise has the mathematical type of a multi-input
   composition;
2. independently equivalent representatives change or destroy a proposed raw
   average;
3. an edge/interior counterfamily preserves every actually registered
   constraint while remaining freely parameterized; and
4. candidate repairs all require additional structure not already selected.

The falsification is premise-scoped: a later native connection, action,
bootstrap equation, or representative selector may close the seam.

## Catch proofs

The verifier must fail when:

1. the Lorentz gauge counterexample is replaced by a non-Lorentz matrix;
2. the midpoint is incorrectly asserted nonsingular;
3. a registered bubble fails to vanish on its claimed vertices/edges/faces;
4. a chart-level choice is mislabeled `DERIVED`;
5. J1 or J2 is silently privileged;
6. an operation is called native while its extra structure is omitted;
7. a premise-source hash changes;
8. the result exceeds the maximum conclusion.

## Independent verification

Load-bearing identities will be recomputed in a separate verifier rather than
read back from the main result. Before banking, a fresh zero-context adversarial
review must inspect the exact package, rerun the verifier, and challenge the
premise-to-conclusion chain.

## Maximum allowed conclusion

At most this audit may state:

- which composition constraints are already `DERIVED`;
- which candidate joins are merely `CHOSE`;
- whether the current registered premises do or do not select a unique native
  complete-coframe composition law;
- which atlas observations are invariant versus join-dependent; and
- the smallest missing mathematical selector, if one is genuinely isolated.

It may not select a physical branch, carrier, action, source, boundary,
cosmology, mass, or scale; canonize a result; authorize GPU work; or turn a
bounded configuration-space observation into realized UDT physics.
