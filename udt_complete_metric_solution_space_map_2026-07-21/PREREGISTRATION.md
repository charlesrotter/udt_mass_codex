# Complete-metric solution-space map — preregistration

Date: 2026-07-21

Base: `fa3d3afda98b2516e58b83a4f383190df9a0aa68`

## Frozen question

What exact research map is required to explore the complete UDT metric algebraically, statically,
time-dependently, and globally without converting a desired physical behavior, a familiar action,
a GR comparison, or a previously tested conditional mechanism into a hidden selector?

This phase plans, designs, and audits that map. It does not execute an algebraic branch solve, ODE
continuation, PDE/time evolution, action variation, empirical comparison, carrier solve, or GPU run.

## Method and source boundary

The map is `METRIC_LED / OBSERVING`, not template-led. Source authority is frozen to:

1. current `LIVE.md`, `HANDOFF.md`, and the corrected scientific frontier;
2. post-July founding Reciprocity, CSN, bootstrap, finite-cell, and premise-reset records;
3. post-July complete-metric/coframe, Cartan, null-line, twist/shear, boundary, and selector audits;
4. frozen native-action adjudication only as a status/variation-domain boundary; and
5. pre-July material only as historical failure, tooling, or counterexample evidence under the
   July-1 provenance firewall—never as affirmative UDT physics.

Generated map records may not influence which source artifacts are admitted or their authority.

## Required distinctions

The map must keep three spaces separate:

- `CONFIGURATION_SPACE`: everything the authorized metric/coframe can express after only genuine
  coordinate, frame, and CSN bookkeeping is identified;
- `CONSTRAINED_SPACE`: configurations satisfying only individually cited UDT premises, signature,
  regularity classes, finite-cell/seal data, and explicitly enumerated global conditions; and
- `DYNAMICAL_SOLUTION_SPACE`: solutions of a separately specified and premise-stamped action,
  equation, or admissibility law. Because no unconditional native dynamics is currently selected,
  this layer must remain branched/conditional rather than silently instantiated.

## Required deliverables

1. authoritative source and correction lineage;
2. complete field/coframe/metric degree-of-freedom census;
3. gauge, coordinate, local-frame, and CSN redundancy ledger;
4. premise, boundary, sign, chart, source, action, and comparison ledger;

8. static and stationary ODE atlas plan;
9. time-live jet, ODE, and eventual PDE atlas plan;

    numerically unresolved outcomes;
12. GR/empirical/carrier comparison firewall applied only after an atlas slice is frozen;
13. anti-shortcut and target-leakage audit;
14. explicit stage gates, rollback/regrade rules, compute estimates, and stop conditions;
15. ten-criterion completeness matrix showing what each future stage covers and drops; and
16. deterministic fail-closed verifier with exercised corruption catches.

## Premise stamps to enforce

Every physical choice must be exactly one of:

- `free-and-explored`;
- `pinned-by-THEORY` with a current citation;
- `pinned-by-OWNER_CLARIFICATION` with a current correction citation;
- `CONDITIONAL_BRANCH` with the branch-defining source;
- `COMPARISON_READOUT_ONLY` with no feedback into selection; or
- `pinned-by-HABIT / BLOCKED` and therefore ineligible for an unqualified solve.

Numerical tolerances, bases, grids, continuation steps, and preconditioners are category-A methods,
but still require soundness, convergence, and raw-residual recording.

## Anti-imposition rules

- No branch is rejected for failing to resemble a particle, lump, spectrum, stable object, GR
  solution, smooth preferred shape, finite mass, or expected cosmology.
- Regularity is recorded by class; it is not a merit filter unless a cited mathematical domain
  explicitly requires it.
- Spherical, diagonal, separable, static, stationary, harmonic, round-screen, zero-shift,
  zero-twist, zero-shear, areal-gauge, supplied topology, and supplied boundary choices are never
  the whole metric. Each is a declared stratum or conditional branch.
- Projector, Hopf, `S2`, `L2+L4`, EH, `C^2`/Bach, GR constraints, WR-L, and empirical targets are
  diagnostics or conditional dynamics only; none may organize the native branch search.
- No linearized result becomes a physical input. Exact jets or controlled diagnostics must retain
  their bounded status.
- No “smallest missing mechanism,” “one thing left,” or selector hunt is permitted during atlas
  construction.

## Preregistered audit questions

1. Does the map begin from an actually authoritative complete metric/coframe, or from a convenient
   post-July representative mislabeled complete?
2. Are lapse, shifts, transverse metric shape, frame rotation, twist, shear, local scale, signed
   `phi`, angular dependence, time dependence, charts, and boundaries all visible?
3. Does any proposed algebraic or ODE stage freeze a degree of freedom whose omitted sector could
   contain emergent structure?
4. Does any boundary or regularity condition enter without current provenance?
5. Does a time-live stage claim physical evolution without a selected native law?
6. Does any GR, empirical, Hopf, carrier, or mass diagnostic feed back into branch acceptance?
7. Can every reduced slice be located inside the full layer graph and regraded when a premise
   changes?
8. Are solver failures characterized rather than converted into scientific no-go claims?

## Required catch-proofs

The independent verifier must reject at least:

- a missing metric sector or duplicated degree of freedom;
- a symmetry reduction presented as complete coverage;
- an untagged physical pin or `pinned-by-HABIT` solve authorization;
- a conditional action labeled native;
- a time-dependent configuration labeled physical evolution without dynamics;
- a GR/readout quantity used as a selection score;
- a result schema that drops singular, divergent, trivial, or unresolved branches;
- a stage that performs comparison before atlas freeze;
- a completeness claim with an uncovered criterion;
- a pre-July source used as affirmative post-firewall physics; and
- generated map evidence feeding back into source selection.

## Certification contract

Acceptance requires:

1. complete source/status lineage with correction overlays;
2. one-row-per-degree and one-row-per-premise deterministic ledgers;
3. explicit whole-frame versus reduced-stratum relationships;
4. algebraic, ODE, time-live, global, and comparison stages with entry/exit/stop gates;
5. no scientific equation solve or physical branch verdict in this package;
6. independent standard-library verification and exercised catch-proofs;
7. repository, frozen-manifest, prior-package, navigation, test-baseline, and original dirty-checkout
   metadata gates; and
8. Charles review before any atlas execution dispatch.
