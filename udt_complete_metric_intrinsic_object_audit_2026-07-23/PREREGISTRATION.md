## Whole question

After quotienting the complete registered UDT metric description by allowed
coframe changes and Common-Scale Neutrality, does the metric and its
registered finite-cell data determine any intrinsic:

1. reduction of the tangent, coframe, conformal-frame, or associated bundle;
2. section or multisection of the conditional celestial null-direction
   sphere bundle;
3. parallel/holonomy-preserved subbundle or tensor; or
4. characteristic, primary, or secondary bundle invariant

that carries the reciprocal representation and/or the Hopf structure without
choosing a coframe, carrier, action, topology, or desired particle result?

This is an object/type census. It is not a search for a Hopfion.
