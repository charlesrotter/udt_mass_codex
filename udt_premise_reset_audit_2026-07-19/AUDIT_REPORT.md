## What caused the regression

Context compaction contributed, but it was an amplifier rather than the root cause.

The root cause was **semantic overloading followed by narrow proof inheritance**:

1. `phi` was used as a signed local field, an additive comparison coordinate, a frame label, and
   sometimes a nonnegative distance depth.
2. A correct objection to negative physical distance led to an overcorrection: replacing signed
   local `phi` by nonnegative `rho` in clock statements.
3. `X_max` began as a universal derivation target but was then supplied symbolically as an input to
   positional-frame calculations.
4. Exact verification established algebra inside each declared model. Later work sometimes treated
   that as verification that the variables had the right UDT meaning.
5. Conversational owner meanings were less durable than frozen package prose. Compaction made that
   imbalance worse, but disk evidence already contained incompatible branches and conventions.

Thus the work spawned disciplined mathematical rabbit holes around under-audited nouns. The
mathematics often remains useful; the cumulative physical story does not.
