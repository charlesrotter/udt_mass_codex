- `c_E` is the measured terrestrial/solar clock-length anchor.

  dimensions only. This audit does not choose either formula, coefficient, or physical role.
- The cold packet's stronger statement that a normalization later identified as `G` must emerge is
  superseded for this audit by the owner clarification. What remains open is the native placement
  and use of observed `G`, not its empirical acceptance.
