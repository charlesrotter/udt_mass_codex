# Clean rederivation priority after the premise reset

This is a sequencing recommendation, not authorization to begin a derivation.

## Priority zero: preserve meanings

Every future calculation must keep five different objects visibly separate:

1. finite ordinary-regime baseline `c_E`;
2. measured observational gravitational anchor `G_obs` at the calibrated physical layer;
3. signed local field `phi(p)`;
4. signed ordered comparison between two locations/observers; and
5. nonnegative metric distance along a specified path.

No symbol may change roles inside a derivation. `X_max` may appear as an unknown global output, but
not as a supplied physical ruler used to prove its own frame invariance.

## First scientific audit: asymptotic-boundary lineage

Before new equations, reconstruct the strongest existing boundary examples—especially the WR-L
family and finite-cell fold records—and answer only:

- which metric and action premises each example used;
- whether the limiting surface is the CMB fold, a distinct cap, or a chart endpoint;
- whether `phi=0` and `phi -> +/-infinity` refer to different surfaces, branches, or conventions;
- whether the limiting coordinate `X` is the same object as global `X_max`;
- which time, ruler, curvature, proper-volume, and mass quantities are actually asymptotic; and
- whether mass dilation was derived or merely interpreted.

This is the highest-priority audit because it can recover real prior work without inventing a new
mechanism and because it controls the meaning of `X_max`.

## Second scientific derivation: global closure unknowns

Only after the boundary lineage is clean, formulate the simultaneous unknowns rather than solving
one from the others circularly:

- complete metric representative/solution;
- global `X_max`;
- total proper volume;
- total mass ledger;
- `rho_tot=M_tot/V_tot`;
- ordinary baseline `c_E`;
- observational anchor `G_obs`, without assuming an action form; and
- any boundary/asymptotic regularity equations genuinely supplied by the metric.

The maximum result would be a closed equation count and dependency graph, not a numerical value.

## Deferred until those two gates pass

- projective or fractional-linear observer-frame laws;
- dynamic and accelerating `X_max` frames;
- identification of the Hopf angular coordinate with physical position;
- local density/source effects;
- complete action or carrier emergence; and
- GPU solution-space searches.

The exact algebra in those lanes is preserved as conditional mathematical material. It is not a
foundation for further physics until the semantic joins above are derived.
