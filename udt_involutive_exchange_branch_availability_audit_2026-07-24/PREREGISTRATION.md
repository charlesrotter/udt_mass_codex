A family passes only if every gate marked `REQUIRED` in
`PASS_GATE_SCHEMA.tsv` is supported by load-bearing evidence from that same
family or by an explicitly recorded dependency whose authority is compatible.

branches, variation domains, boundary choices, carriers, or lifecycle states.

Gate values are exactly:

- `YES`
- `NO`
- `CONDITIONAL`
- `OPEN`
- `NOT_APPLICABLE`

Only `YES` satisfies a required gate.
