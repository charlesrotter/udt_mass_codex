# Owner-stated principle — global bootstrap and narrow matter-existence density

**Date:** 2026-07-15

**Authority:** Charles

**Effect:** new working principle for derivation; no action, carrier, density value, or canon entry is
silently supplied

## Owner statement

> The UDT universe bootstraps itself. Matter can only exist within an extremely narrow range of the
> total mass density of the universe.

## Prospective status

\[
\boxed{
\begin{gathered}
\text{The realized UDT universe is a self-consistent global solution, not a background chosen first.}\\
\text{A globally self-consistent matter-bearing solution exists only in a narrow total-density window.}
\end{gathered}
}
\]

This is recorded as an **OWNER-STATED WORKING PRINCIPLE** pending any later canon action. “Narrow” is
not assigned a numerical width here. The theory must derive its center and width before observational
comparison.

## Interpretation ledger

- **Total mass** means the complete UDT ledger: localized matter, horizon/black-hole contribution,
  diffuse or geometric reservoir, and any other term derived from the eventual action. It does not
  mean visible/localized matter alone.
- **Total density** means total mass divided by the proper volume of the complete realized spatial
  cell/slice. The full time-live volume and boundary are not yet derived.
- **Primary global reading:** local carrier equations may possess mathematical solutions outside the
  window, but no complete self-consistent UDT universe containing physical matter does.
- **Stronger local reading:** no stable localized carrier solution exists outside the window. This is
  kept as a separate fork; it is not silently inferred from the word “bootstraps.”
- **No nonlocal insertion:** the global average density may not be written directly into a local
  action as a fitted coupling or hard cutoff. Its influence must arrive through the solved physical
  geometry, boundary data, or a separately derived global variational condition.
- **Recycling is redistribution:** conversion among ledger sectors does not change total density when
  total mass and proper volume are fixed.

## Falsifiable core

Let \(\mathcal W\) be the set of dimensionless total densities for which the full nonlinear UDT
equations, boundary conditions, global closure, and physical stability gates admit a matter-bearing
solution. The principle requires

\[
\mathcal W\ne\varnothing,
\qquad
\frac{\operatorname{width}(\mathcal W)}{\operatorname{center}(\mathcal W)}\ll1.
\]

It fails if the complete theory admits matter-bearing universes over a broad continuum, admits none,
or obtains a narrow window only by inserting its location/width by hand.
